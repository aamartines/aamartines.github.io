About

I'm a freelance web designer and Instructional Technology Specialist for Smith College. I have a dogged persistence and delight in solving problems, believe anyone is capable of picking up a few new tech tricks, and find odd enjoyment in writing documentation.

Previously, I studied Learning, Media and Technology at the University of Massachusetts Amherst while supporting ambitious faculty with using technology in education through Academic Computing and teaching professional development courses in computer skills with the Labor/Management Workplace Education program.

A lover of all things fashion, I'm also a board game junkie who loves cooking up new recipes, tweeting, and anything written by Brandon Sanderson.
